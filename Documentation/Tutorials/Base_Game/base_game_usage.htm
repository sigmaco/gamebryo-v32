Base Game Demo Usage and Controls

In the Base Game demo, you can control the Avatar's movements and perform certain actions depending on the room in which the Avatar is located.

Room 1

  • Move the avatar into red circle on the floor to activate trigger.
  • The trigger is sticky, once activated it remains activated for about 10 seconds.
  • When the trigger activates, door opens, proceed through doorway.

Room 2

  • The trigger in this room is non-sticky.
  • Move the avatar to the box in the corner, pick it up using the N key or the B button on a console.
  • Move the avatar into the red circle on the floor, drop the box in the red circle using the M key or the A button on a console.
  • The box in the circle will activate the trigger and hold it active.
  • The door will open, proceed through the doorway.

Room 3

  • The trigger in this room is the treasure chest in the corner.
  • Avoid the roaming AI entities in this room. Contact with one will reset the avatar's position back to the previous room.
  • Move the avatar to the treasure chest to remove the AI entities from the room and to open the door.
  • Proceed through the doorway.

Room 4

  • There are no triggers in this room, just collect all the boxes by colliding with them.
  • Press ESC key to exit application.

The following table outlines the keyboard and game pad controls.

Keyboard Game Pad Action
UP_ARROW, DOWN_ARROW Left Analog Forward, Backward Move the Avatar forward, backward
LEFT_ARROW, RIGHT_ARROW Left Analog Left, Right Move the Avatar left, right
W/w Right Analog Forward Zoom the Camera In.
S/s Right Analog Back Zoom the Camera Out.
A/a Right Analog Left Orbit the Camera counter-clockwise.
D/d Right Analog Right Orbit the Camera clockwise.
M/m A button Drop the Box
N/n B button Pickup the box
F11 Right Analog Push Toggle Visual Tracker
Space   Skip splash screen
ESC Start & Select/Back buttons Quit the Application
