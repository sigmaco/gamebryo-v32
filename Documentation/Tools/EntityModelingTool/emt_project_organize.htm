Organizing Items within your Projects

You can have many different models, presets, and diagrams within your projects. It is important to organize the content so that it is manageable and you can easily find the specific files you are looking for.

You can organize the content in your project by arranging it into folders, just like you do with the files on your computer. You can create folders within folders to build a logical hierarchy of the content in your project. For example, you can organize the content in your project by the different levels or zones within your game.

To organize the content within your projects using folders

  1. In the EMT Project Explorer, right-click the open project node, a virtual folder in the Folders sub-pane, or empty space in the Contents sub-pane.
  2. From the context menu, click Add > New Folder.

    A new folder is added to the project.

  3. Enter a name for the new folder.
  4. Drag one or more related items into this new folder.
  5. Continue to organize your content by adding more folders and dragging the corresponding models, presets, and diagrams into them.
