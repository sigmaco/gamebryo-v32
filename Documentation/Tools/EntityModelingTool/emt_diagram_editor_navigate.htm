Navigating in the Mix-in/Diagram Editor

When defining mix-ins and creating diagrams, you can have many different models and presets within the editor. At different points in the process, you may want to get a strategic view of the entire mix-in/diagram and at other times you may want to focus in on a specific model or group of models. For this reason, both editors have zoom and scroll tools. These tools allow you to quickly and easily navigate the models and presets within the diagram.

To navigate the Mix-in/Diagram Editor

  • In the EMT Project Explorer, do one of the following:
    • Double-click a model or preset to load it into the Mix-in Editor.
    • Double-click a diagram to load it into the Diagram Editor.
  • In the Diagram Editor, double-clicking a model will open it in the Mix-in Editor.
  • In the Mix-in Editor, double-clicking a model will regenerate the view focused on that model. This is an easy way to explore up or down the mix-in chain.
  • Drag the Zoom slider in the top right corner of the editor to zoom in and out. Drag to the left to zoom out and to the right to zoom in. (The mouse wheel also zooms in and out of the editor.)
  • To center the models use the scroll sliders at the bottom and right side of the editor. In the Diagram Editor, there is also a Zoom To Fit toolbar button. Finally, dragging the middle mouse button will pan the view.
