Laying Out Presets and Models in World Builder

After you create the various presets in your project, you can begin laying out instances of these presets in World Builder. World Builder is a 3D environment where you can place presets and entity models to create a certain area of your world space at time zero.

When you work in World Builder, you have access to the Entity, Palette, and Entity Editor views. These three views are tightly integrated with the 3D viewport to make it easy for you to set up the presets and models in the 3D environment and fine-tune their properties.
