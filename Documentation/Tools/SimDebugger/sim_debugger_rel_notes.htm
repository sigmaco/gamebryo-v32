Known Issues and Limitations

All Gamebryo LightSpeed Releases

  • In multiplayer games, Simulation Debugger is designed to communicate with the host process and receive information about the state of entities from the perspective of the host. Peer-only events are not available for review.
  • Simulation Debugger does not account for rapid iteration on entity models during gameplay. Updates to these models may not be reflected in the reported entity state information.
