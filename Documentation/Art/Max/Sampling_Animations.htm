Sampling Animations in 3ds Max

In some cases, the Gamebryo Max plug-in will be forced to sample the keyframes of an animation. When this occurs, the Gamebryo Max plug-in will use the highest and lowest key frames in the scene graph to sample the animations. In some cases, such as Character Studio bipeds, this too may not be sufficient to properly sample the keyframes. In this case, it is necessary to add a top-level note track entry at the start and end of the sample time range. If animation sequences are used, this will be accomplished automatically.
