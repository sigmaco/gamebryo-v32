Introduction to Animation with 3ds Max

Gamebryo exports animation just as you see it in Max. This section primarily exists to provide artists with additional details about animation export where it is needed.

Animation export is made more powerful with the notion of sequences. Sequences are snippets of an animation that are written to a file. To export these sequences in to *.KF (Key Frame) sequences for Gamebryo to use, see the section on Animation Manager.
