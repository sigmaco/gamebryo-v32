Parallax Map in Max

The "Parallax" map field holds a texture that simulates displacement mapping by altering the texture coordinates of all textures sharing the same UV channel so that the surface seems to have more curvature. It is important to note that all normal maps are tangent-space in the Gamebryo standard pipeline. Note that this method requires the existence of Normal, Binormal and Tangent (NBT) frames, much like Normal Mapping.
