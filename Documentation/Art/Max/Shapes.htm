Shapes and 3ds Max

Shape or spline objects can be exported from Max as NiMesh objects in Gamebryo under the condition that in Max, they contain the string "NiExportLines" in their user-defined properties. This is to keep unwanted lines from exporting.

Only shapes from the Splines category of Max objects can be exported. NURBS Curves are not supported.

When a spline is exported, it uses the wire color parameter from Max as an emissive color in Gamebryo so that its appearance is consistent. No other material properties are supported when exporting splines as NiMesh objects.
