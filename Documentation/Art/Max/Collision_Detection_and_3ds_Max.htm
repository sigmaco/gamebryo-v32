Collision Detection and 3ds Max

In 3ds Max, Gamebryo enables collision data to be specified as needed. The type of collision method, as well as how the scene graph propagation should occur may be specified.

Typically, ABVs (Alternate Bounding Volumes) will be generated during export by way of the CreateABV plug-in. ABVs may be generated from proxy box geometry, on export from 3ds Max, by using the naming scheme described in the next section, and by editing the export "Process Script" such that it includes the CreateABV plug-in. (The appropriate options must also be checked.) See the documentation on CreateABV for more information.
