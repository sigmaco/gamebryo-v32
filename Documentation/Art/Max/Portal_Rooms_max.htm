PortalRooms.max

The PortalRooms.max sample demonstrates an indoor, multi-room, portal-tagged dataset that was created with the Portal Utility. The scene contains several animated predefined cameras. These cameras illustrate how having a portal setup can help cull obscured objects in the scene. To see an example of portal culling, switch to one of the animated cameras (NorthCam, WestCam, or SouthEastCam) and toggle the rendering mode between normal and wireframe. In normal mode, everything appears to draw as it is supposed to, but in wireframe you can see objects disappearing once they are out of the line of sight that is created by the portal.

Note that Viewing the scene with the Default Camera created by SceneViewer will display a blank screen because the Default Camera will be outside the room group.
