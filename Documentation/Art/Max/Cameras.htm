Cameras and 3ds Max

The Gamebryo Max plug-in supports many of the options available in Max Cameras.

Parameters

Orthographic projection is supported. Please use resolutions whose aspect ratios are 4:3 to get the most accurate conversion.

Manual clipping planes are supported. Other parameters such as Lens size and FOV are supported via the frustum that gets created for the camera.

Target and Free cameras are supported.

All other options are not supported.

Depth Of Field Parameters

This rollout is not supported.
