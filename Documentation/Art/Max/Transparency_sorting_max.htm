Transparency sorting.max

The transparency sorting.max sample contains two glasses. One glass comprises multiple objects and sorts the best. The other is one object. This latter object still sorts well thanks to Gamebryo's sorting abilities. If you are having inter-object sorting problems, you may need to split your object into more than one piece, though this can negatively influence the triangle/mesh ratio.
