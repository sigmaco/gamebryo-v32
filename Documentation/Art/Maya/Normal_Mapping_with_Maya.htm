Normal Map

A Normal map is a texture in which the normals of a surface are encoded as RGB values. These normals replace the per-vertex normals for per-pixel lighting calculations. Normal maps can greatly increase the graphical appeal of your title. It is important to note that all normal maps are tangent-space in the Gamebryo standard pipeline. This means the Normal, Binormal, and Tangent (NBT) frames must be calculated per-vertex. If the "Binormals and Tangents" method is left at "None", it will default to ATI.
