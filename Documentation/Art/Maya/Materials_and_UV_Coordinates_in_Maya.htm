Materials and UV Coordinates in Maya

The Gamebryo Maya exporter captures these texture placement attributes:

  • Wrap U, Wrap V—represent the texture wrapping behavior
  • Repeat UV—determine the texture tiling values
  • Offset—used to move the texture on the mesh surface
  • Rotation—Rotate the texture around its center.

Inside Gamebryo, these attributes will be represented by a texture transform. The attributes can be animated and they make it easy for multiple maps to share the same UV set.
