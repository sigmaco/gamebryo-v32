Introduction to the Gamebryo Tools Shelf in Maya

The Gamebryo Maya Plug-in creates a shelf within the Maya interface. You can use the buttons on this shelf to access most of the Gamebryo features and settings directly. You can also launch the export and viewing processes.

The following buttons are available on the Gamebryo tools shelf:
