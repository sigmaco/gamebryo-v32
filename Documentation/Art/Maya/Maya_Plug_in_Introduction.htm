Maya Plug-in: Introduction

Welcome to the Gamebryo Maya Plug-in. This manual gives you information, hints, and tips on how to produce great content using the Gamebryo Maya Plug-in inside Maya.

This documentation provides background information useful for artists to decide on geometric limitations, visual effects that work best with most graphics cards, conversions from Maya to Gamebryo, and the best way to achieve an overall satisfying result while avoiding choices that may slow your real-time content down.

To maximize your ability to create great content, it is a good idea to read the Artist's Manual cover to cover, but it is also useful for quick reference. Please read Installation and Setup, first.
