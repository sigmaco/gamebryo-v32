Setting Up Skin LOD

You can use Skin LOD to determine what skins are associated with different Bone LOD levels. Just as you can associate different groups of bones with a particular LOD, you can associate a skin with Bone LOD levels as well. The highest LOD may have a complex skin, but you might want to associate a simpler mesh with the lower levels of detail to further simplify drawing. It is important to remember that Bone LOD and Skin LOD work together. If you are going to use either, you need to use both.

Look in the Maya Artist Examples directory for examples of Gamebryo LOD in Maya.

To set up skin LOD

  1. Select the most detailed character skin and click the Skin LOD button.
  2. In the Gamebryo Skin LOD dialog, you can see the range of Bone LODs that are associated with this skin. Set your minimum and maximum accordingly and click the Set Bone LOD Level button.
  3. Keep repeating steps 1 and 2 until you have assigned all of your skins to Bone LOD ranges. Every Bone LOD should have an associated skin and Skin LODs should not share any Bone LOD levels.
