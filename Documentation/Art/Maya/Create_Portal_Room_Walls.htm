Create Portal Room Walls

Click the Create Portal Room Walls button to create axis-aligned walls that completely encompass the selected portal room. During the export, the portal conversion code detects a wall by locating "_NDLWL" in the name. For your convenience, a special NiMultiShader material named "PortalWallShader" is applied to the created walls. This makes them mostly transparent so you can easily see both your room and its walls.

Create Portal Room Walls button

There are a few thing you need to remember when you create portal walls.

  • The normal defined by the plane in Maya MUST point INTO the room.
  • The room and its contents MUST be completely enclosed by the portal walls. If they do not, you will need to break up the geometry in Maya.
  • Two rooms must not overlap. It is acceptable for the walls of two room to intersect but you will get a warning. If the rooms overlap you will likely see a visible flash as you move from one to the other.
