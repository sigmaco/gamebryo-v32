Introduction to Exporting from Maya

Exporting from Maya begins with traversing the scene graph and converting Maya nodes into the corresponding Gamebryo nodes. Gamebryo tries to emulate as close to what you see in Maya is what you get in Gamebryo as possible. Unfortunately, the two systems do not always match. If you wish to ignore certain parts of the scene graph, you can place the nodes under a transform node with the name "GamebryoIgnore". This provides an easy way for not exporting out scale models of custom rig controllers. This will simplify the export process and give a more optimal result.
