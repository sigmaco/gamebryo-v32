Animating Cameras through the Gamebryo Maya Plug-in

Maya's camera attributes are preserved during export. Exported Gamebryo NiCameras honor the following Maya attributes:

  • Near Clipping Plane
  • Far Clipping Plane
  • Aspect Ratio
  • Field of View

Note

Aspect Ratio and Field of View parameters are also connected to the Focal Length and Camera Aperture parameters.

The Gamebryo Maya Plug-in will optionally export Maya's default cameras. You can animate the position and rotation of cameras, but the other camera attributes, such as near plane, focal length, and so on are not currently supported by the Gamebryo animation system.
