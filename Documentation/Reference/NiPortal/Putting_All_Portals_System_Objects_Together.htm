Non-Convex Rooms in Portalled Scenes

Since each NiRoom object must bound a convex space in 3D, a physical space in a scene that is not convex cannot be represented by a single NiRoom object. However, this does not mean that every physical "room" in the interior scene must be convex. Non-convex rooms can be represented as the union of two or more convex NiRoom objects, joined by a portal that fills the entire opening between them. See below for an example.
