D3D11Renderer Shutdown

There is no concept of a global initialization or shutdown of the D3D11 rendering system. Deleting the renderer will automatically free most D3D11 resources, and the remainder will be freed when the application exits (assuming that the application has not specifically made references to D3D11 objects). One very important note is that the D3D11 renderer requires that all textures and geometry be destroyed prior to renderer shutdown. This includes any geometry (including screen-space geometry) that was drawn by the renderer, and all rendered textures and cube maps.
