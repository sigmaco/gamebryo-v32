LogoApplication Usage Guidelines

The LogoApplication provides you with a way to easily incorporate the Gamebryo logo into your project. You can extend the demo to display multiple logos in a similar fashion. Though the LogoApplication is extensible and configurable, you should follow the guidelines below to display the Gamebryo logo.

  • Non-vector-based Emergent or Gamebryo logo assets should not be resized if resizing causes pixelation
  • The logo aspect ratio must be maintained (logo cannot be stretched or squashed)
  • Colors cannot be substituted (for assistance, see Gamebryo Identity Guidelines or contact Emergent)
  • Fade-in / Fade-out duration
    • Default: 2 seconds fade in, 2 seconds fade out
    • Required: No (but must match display of other logos)
    • Customizable: Yes (but duration must match display of other logos)
  • Grow / Shrink
    • Default: 3%
    • Required: No (but must match display of other logos)
    • Customizable: Yes (but must match display of other logos)
