LogoApplication Overview

The LogoApplication demonstrates how to display the Gamebryo logo on multiple platforms. You may extend this demo to display your own logo. The sample is designed to calculate the aspect ratio and display the logo's square texture scaled to occupy 95% of the display.

Note

Most Gamebryo licensees have a contractual obligation to display the Gamebryo logo during startup. If you have questions about your contract, contact your Developer Relations Manager.

Known Issues

The Gamebryo logo appears slightly pixelated on the PS3 when run in 480 16 x 9 mode. This is not a common resolution and all standard resolutions display appropriately. If you need to ship a title that must support this mode, contact Emergent Game Technologies support. We will supply you with a logo texture that supports that resolution.
