Metal Wars Sample Usage and Controls

General Usage

  • When the demo starts it renders two "splash screens" in sequence; one while loading and one when ready to run. Once the "ready to run" screen is displayed, the user can hit any input control and the demo will start.
  • After 30 seconds of idle time, the demo switches to "killbot focus" mode with an animated camera focusing on the detail of the killbot. Any input switches out of idle mode.
Keyboard Game Pad Action
W/w Left Analog Move character in his facing direction
S/s Left Analog Move character backwards
A/a Left Analog Sidestep left
D/d Left Analog Sidestep right
Mouse Right Analog Controls camera/character look at direction, i.e. yaw and pitch for left-right and up-down mouse movement, respectively.
Left Mouse Button A Button Fire robot blaster.
Mouse Wheel or '.' and ',' Right and Left Triggers Slides the camera closer to the robot (roll wheel forward) and further away from the character (roll wheel backwards). Sliding the camera very close to the character will cause the view to switch to first-person mode.
Right Mouse Button Y Button Toggle through the different HUD modes in first-person mode.
I N/A Force camera to idle mode.
UP_ARROW, DOWN_ARROW LEFT_UP, LEFT_DOWN Prev/Next menu item (when menu is active)
LEFT_ARROW, RIGHT_ARROW LEFT_LEFT, LEFT_RIGHT Dec/Inc menu item value (when menu is active)
ENTER RIGHT_DOWN Toggle menu item value (when menu is active)
ESC Start Button Quit or toggle menu on/off, depending on build.
