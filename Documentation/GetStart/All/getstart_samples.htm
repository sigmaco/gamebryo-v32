Running Gamebryo LightSpeed Samples

Gamebryo LightSpeed ships with samples for supported platforms. For PC, the sample executables are installed to %EMERGENT_PATH%\Samples. You can launch most samples from the Start menu, using either the Sample Browser or shortcuts under GameDemos or TechDemos.
