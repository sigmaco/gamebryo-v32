Categories: Introduction

In Gamebryo LightSpeed, a category is a conceptual tag, label or delivery address that is applied to local or network messages. You can use categories to partition, control, and enhance the performance of messaging and entity interactions in your LightSpeed-based application. By partitioning groups of messages into categories, you can reduce the number of irrelevant messages that the message system will send. This reduces the processing that your code needs to do. Categorizing messages and data in Gamebryo Lightspeed can also limit the information that a client receives in order to reduce bandwidth and enhance fairness and security in an online game.
