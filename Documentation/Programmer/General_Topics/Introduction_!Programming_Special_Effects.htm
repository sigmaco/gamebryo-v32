Introduction to Programming Special Effects

These topics describe many of the special, dynamic effects that are supported by Gamebryo. While most of the effects can be generated in Gamebryo content directly out of the major modeling packages, they can also be created and modified in application code. The major category of special effects described in this manual are lighting and texturing effects, which are represented directly in the scene graph via the NiDynamicEffect class and its numerous subclasses.
