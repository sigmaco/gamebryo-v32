Overview of Programming for High Performance

This section describes numerous ways that developers can use knowledge of their data (as well as other game-specific shortcuts) to optimize performance of their datasets and application(s) with Gamebryo.  Optimization methods are described, and references to more detailed documentation pertaining to other optimizations (in some cases, per platform) are included.

One of the most important, and one of the most overlooked, considerations for building a high-performance application is to pay attention to performance from the very beginning of the project through to the very end of the project. Performance impact should always be a factor in every design decision, whether in code or in artwork. Further, frequent performance assessments, analysis, and corrective measures throughout the development of the product are critical to success. Attempting to correct serious performance issues during the endgame of product development rarely succeeds.

This section describes the methods, tools, and techniques for measuring and analyzing performance, and avoiding or correcting performance issues in code. Other sections do the same for art assets built with 3ds max or Maya.
